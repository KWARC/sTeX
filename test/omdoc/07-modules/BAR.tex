A module that contributes a symvariant 
\begin{module}[name=BAR]
  \symdef{BAR}{BAR}
  \symdef[arity=0]{barbar}{\psi}
  \notation{barbar}[1]{\barbar[arity=0](#1)}
  \notation[arity=0,cap]{barbar}{\Psi}
  \notation[cap]{barbar}[1]{\barbar[arity=0,cap](#1)}

  And it talks about these things a $\BAR$ and a $\barbar{x}$ and its variant $\barbar[cap]{x}$.
\end{module}
